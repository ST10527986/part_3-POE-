safe Haven ZA is a leading South African animal shelter (NGO) which was founded in 2018 with a simple but clear and powerful vision to provide safe, loving homes for refugeed animals off the street. What started as a small volunteer based start up quickly picked up steam across local Cape tonian communities as the animal welfare issues increased across local communities. Pet Haven has grown into a well established operation including veterinary care, sterilization programs, and adoption initiatives. Pet Haven ZA’s mission is to create a safe and compassionate environment in South Africa for all animals. They are dedicated to this task and will do so by rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming lost or abandoned animals. The Target audience of this NGO are Vulnerable families or individuals looking for their fur buddy or just pet lovers or enthusiasts looking to lend a helping hand in their volunteer program to look after the fur balls all in a day's work.
