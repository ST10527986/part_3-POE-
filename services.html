Safe Haven ZA Provides:

Adoption

Safe Haven ZA as an open adoption center destined for you to go and pick out your future furry friend. Cats, Dogs , rabbits we have it all!

Foster Program

Safe Haven ZA cares immensly for animal welfare, with our foster program we take in all types of lost, abandonded or injured animals.

Veterinary Care

Safe Haven ZA provides Basic check-ups, vaccinations, for emergency situations we strongly urge you to head to your nearest clinic as we do not provide emergency treatment.

Volunteer Opportunities

Join the Safe Haven ZA team to help care for our furry fellows, community outreach program and events.(must be 18+ to apply)

Please use our contact directory link to contact further about either adoptionor, Veterinary care or our foster program and for volunteers please follow our volunteer form.
